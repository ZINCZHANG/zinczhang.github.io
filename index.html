Short Bio

Mr. Yicheng Zhang is a PhD candidate under the supervision of Prof Su Rong in School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU). He is affiliated with Rolls-Royce @NTU Corporate Lab. Mr. Zhang received his Master degree from Division of Automation, School of Information Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2014, and his Bachelor degree from School of Electrical Engineering and Automation, Hefei University of Technology (HFUT) in 2011. Since August 2014, he became a PhD candidate in Nanyang Technological University. His current research interests include optimization method, power scheduling and intelligent transportation systems.

Research Interests

    2. Lagrangian relaxation

  2. Smart Grid

    1. Power allocation and scheduling in smart grid

    2. Optimal power flow

    3. Optimal control design in smart grid

  3. Intelligent transportation systems

    2. Traffic flow management
