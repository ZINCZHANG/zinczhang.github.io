About

I am an intelligent transport researcher. My current work focuses on designing and implementing intelligent mobility solutions, tailored for Singapore’s smart transport systems. I earned my Ph.D. from Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in 2019, and since then, I have been actively involved in several industry and research initiatives supported by agencies such as NRF, A*STAR, EDB, and CAAS. My research interests span intelligent transport systems, transportation electrification, and the application of optimization and machine learning techniques to solve real-world problems. Recently, I have been leading efforts to apply optimization and machine learning-based methods to challenges in the air traffic management and aeronautical domains, bridging research innovation with practical industry needs.

Research Interests

  • Intelligent Transportation Systems: Modeling and Optimizations
  • Traffic Signal Scheduling in Large-scale networks
  • Optimal Traffic Assignment Problems
  • Congestion Reduction based on Macroscopic Fundamental Diagrams
  • Optimizations in Public Transportation
